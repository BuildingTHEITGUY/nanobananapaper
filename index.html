PaperBanana: Automating Academic Illustration

Mohamed Asath (Project Lead)
University of Dubai • IT Infrastructure & DevOps Initiative
Original Paper (Google) Code Pilot

Executive Summary

Generating publication-ready illustrations is a critical bottleneck in the University of Dubai's research workflow. While our faculty excels at discovery, the manual labor of formatting diagrams often delays submission.

We introduce the PaperBanana Agentic Framework to our institutional infrastructure. Unlike standard AI tools, this system orchestrates specialized agents to retrieve references, plan content, and write executable Python code for statistical plots. This ensures 100% data fidelity and "NeurIPS-standard" aesthetics, directly supporting our goal of increasing acceptance rates in top-tier journals.
